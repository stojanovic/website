Exercises

Workshop

You are a team of front end developers working on a web application for meetups (think meetup.com). But you don't have any backend developers, so you'll need to do everything alone. And you need to do that ASAP, of course.

What

Your task is to create a simple forntend, you know how to do CSS and things like that, so you want to focus on functionality first. Then you need to create a backend for your app, which will allow your users to sign up, log in, list meetups, add a new meetup, and edit or delete an existing one.

How

You have several friends mentioning some new cloud computing concept called serverless. From their story, it seems that serverless thingy simplifies the work on the backend, and can help you to develop your backennd.

There's one more thing, they have an opportunity to show the app on a popular podcast that can bring thousands of new users. Your team will get a nice bonus if you make the backend scalable to support the high traffic. Are you ready for a challenge?

Well, you don't have anything to lose, let's try it!
