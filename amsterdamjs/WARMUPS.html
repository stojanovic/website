Warmups

Get to know each other

Before we begin, spent some time to meet your team. You should do the following:

  1. Introduce yourself to the table, let the team know why are you here and what do you want to learn today.
  2. Discuss what is serverless and how it can be beneficial to frontend developers with your team. Then we'll pick one person from each team to give a short explanation to the whole group.
