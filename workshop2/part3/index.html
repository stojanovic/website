Part 3: build a production-ready backend

Your team presented the application to stakeholders, and they were happy with it, congratulations!

But your product manager has a bad news. The external team didn't finish the API, and your client desperately needs an MVP ready ASAP, because they need to present the app to their investors. They asked your team if you can convert your mock API to the production ready backend for the meetup app.

There's one more thing, they have an opportunity to show the app on a popular podcast that can bring thousands of new users. Your team will get a nice bonus if you make the backend scalable to support the high traffic. Are you ready for a challenge?
